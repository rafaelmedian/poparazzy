This application challenges the current meanings of “identity”, a complicated and unclear concept that nonetheless plays a central role in our sense of self.In the year 2020, where totality of one’s self construal oscillates between digital and physical presence, digital identity performance is often misconstrued as fake, while physical ones are authentic and real. But just as a prism contorts light into rainbows, our digital presence merely refracts our identity into an even wider spectrum of perspectives.
‍
We built this application to help navigate the puzzle of identity and offer a new framework that digital presence can play in shaping our perception of each other and ourselves.
